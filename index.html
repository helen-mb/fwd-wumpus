Hunt the Wumpus

Introduction

It's either hunt or be hunted.

Your bunker has been breached. Your colony was already small. Now it's... tiny.
There's only one way out, and you are the only person with some fight left in you.

With only a bow and arrow and a faulty pair of infrared goggles,
will you take the fate of the colony in your hands?

Instructions

Objective:

Your mission is to hunt down the Wumpus and eliminate it. Be cautious, as dangers await you in the dark!


Game Map:

The game is played in a network of interconnected rooms. Each room is numbered, and tunnels connect the rooms. The rooms are filled with hazards such as bottomless pits, bats, and the deadly Wumpus.


Controls:

To navigate the map, click on a "Room Number" button to move into that room. By default, your action is set to "moving", so all you have to do is choose the room you want to investigate.

To shoot the Wumpus, you must first prepare your arrow. Press the "Prepare Arrow" button, then click a "Room Number" button to specify which room you want to shoot into. Finally, click the "Shoot Arrow" button to release your arrow.

To cancel the "shoot" action, click the "Move" button.


Hazards:

PIT: One of the rooms contains a bottomless pit. If you enter a room with a pit, you'll fall and lose the game.

Bats: One of the rooms contains "super bats." They can pick you up and drop you in a random room.

Wumpus: The Wumpus is deadly. If you enter the cave where the Wumpus is, you might be eaten and lose the game. If it does not eat you, it will move to a neighboring room.


Warnings:

Stench: You'll smell a stench if the Wumpus is in an adjacent cave.

Breeze: You'll feel a breeze if there is a pit in an adjacent cave.

Screeching: You'll hear a screeching if bats are in an adjacent cave.

Pay attention to warnings (stench, breeze, screeching) to deduce the locations of hazards.


Winning:

Eliminate the Wumpus by shooting it with an arrow to win the game. Be cautious and use your wits to navigate the rooms and avoid hazards!

Good Luck.
